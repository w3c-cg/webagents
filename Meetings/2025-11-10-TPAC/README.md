# WebAgents TPAC Agenda and Organizational Details

TPAC 2025 will take place on November 10-14 as a hybrid event (Kobe, Japan & online). Please 
refer to the [TPAC website](https://www.w3.org/2025/11/TPAC/) for further details. 

The WebAgents Community Group (CG) will meet on Monday, November 10, from 15:30 to 16:45 JST (Kobe local time) / 07:30 to 08:45 CET.

You can check the time in your local zone here: [https://zonestamp.toolforge.org/1762756200](https://zonestamp.toolforge.org/1762756200).

See the [calendar entry](https://www.w3.org/events/meetings/cf8f76b2-8756-44f5-9299-fb39cf5ee66a/) for details on the meeting location and connection options.
